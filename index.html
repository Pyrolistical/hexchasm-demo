    How to play

    • A new puzzle arrives every day.
    • Words are hidden along the lines connecting letters. Trace one by tapping adjacent letters or swiping across them, then release to submit.
    • Every word is at least 4 letters long.
    • Finding a solution word removes its lines from the board. Clear every solution word to finish the puzzle.
    • Other dictionary words you stumble on count as bonus words — fun, but not required.
    • Stuck? Hold Reveal solution for a second to see the remaining words.
